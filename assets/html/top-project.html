Upstream Travel is a travel app that helps users in find their dream travel destination. This app not only provides recommendations for restaurants and interesting sights, but also presents updated safety data and security ratings for thousands of cities and neighborhoods world wide. The safety ratings are based on six categories: women's safety, health, physical threats, theft, political freedom and LGBTQ safety.
